Rahul Aswani is a Developer, Product Manager, Entrepreneur and Startup Evangalist. He started his career as a Software Developer in 2014 and since then has moved acorss domains and functions like Engineering, Product, Strategy and Growth and has seen the entire cycle of building a startup and scalling it.

He has worked at Ola as a Software Developer and later as a Product Manager of the Consumer Facing Ola app where he worked on improving Customer Experience and building features focused on Indian Consumers around Indian problems, one such feature is Ola Offline which enables Indian Ola users to book a cab even without internet.

Apart from work rahul is also actively involved in Bangalore's startup community and used to manager Bangalore Startups and Bangalore Startups Connect Facebook groups which together have 120,000 aspiring Entrepreneurs, SMBs, Freelancers, developers, Product Managers, MArketers and Startup enthusiasts.
