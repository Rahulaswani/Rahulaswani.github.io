Rahul Aswani is a Product Manager, Entrepreneur and Startup Evangalist with deep experience in building both consumer facing and supply management products in Location based on demand mobile logistics and transportation space.

He started his career as a Software Developer and since then has moved acorss domains and functions like Engineering, Product, Strategy and Growth and has seen the entire life cycle of building a startup and scalling it.

In his current role as Product Manager of Driver Platform at Go-Jek, Rahul oversees the Product development of Driver Platform which is a suite of 4 driver apps used by thousands of Go-Jek drivers to deliver millions of orders everyday.

Prior to Go-Jek he has worked at Ola as a Software Developer and later as a Product Manager of the Consumer Facing Ola app where he worked on improving Customer Experience and building features focused on Indian Consumers around Indian problems, one such feature is Ola Offline which enables Indian Ola users to book a cab even without internet.

He is also actively involved in Bangalore's startup community and used to manager Bangalore Startups and Bangalore Startups Connect Facebook groups which together have 120,000 aspiring Entrepreneurs, SMBs, Freelancers, developers, Product Managers, MArketers and Startup enthusiasts.
